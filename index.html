Descriptions:



DORAEMON is a fictional character
in the Japanese Manga and Anime
series of the same name created by
FIJIKO F. FUJIO. Doraemon is
a MALE ROBOTIC EARLESS CAT
that travels back in time from the
22nd Century TO AID A PRETEEN
BOY NAMED NOBITA. The Genre
of this Manga is Comedy and Science fiction.
